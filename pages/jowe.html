Jowe, also known by his nickname "The Negosh", is the enigmatic server owner of the Discord. Known for his strategic silences and uncanny ability to bait rage in the most composed users, Jowe has become a legend in his own right within the community.

Despite holding the role of Server Owner, Jowe is often described as a "hands-off leader," preferring to watch the chaos unfold rather than directly intervene. His cryptic presence is marked by his trademark greeting — "Hello, Hello" — usually followed by total radio silence.

Many believe Jowe’s Twitch alias, TheNegotiatorace, is where his true personality shines. Those brave enough to engage in a voice chat with him often leave with more questions than answers.

He joined the server on May 21, 2021, and since then has played a vital role in shaping the server's culture — for better or worse.

Background

Jowe joined the Discord on May 21, 2021. Little is known about his early days, but his rise to Server Owner was marked by a mix of mystery and mild chaos. His leadership style is as unconventional as his sporadic appearances.

Personality

Often silent but always watching, Jowe is best described as a master of the long game. He rarely speaks, but when he does, it’s either to bait a reaction or deliver a cryptic one-liner. His favorite greeting — "Hello, Hello" — is now feared across channels.

Online Presence

Under the Twitch alias TheNegotiatorace, Jowe streams on occasion. However, the streams are just as unpredictable as he is, often starting with long idle periods or spontaneous rants about controller aim assist.
