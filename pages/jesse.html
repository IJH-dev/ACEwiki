Jesse, known in the server as Jesse1k, is a certified wild card. Whether it’s throwing outlandish takes, gambling on impossible odds, or vanishing for days, Jesse’s presence is always felt — even when he’s not around. He lives up to his nickname "The Risk Taker" with questionable bets and even more questionable luck.

Despite his chaotic energy, Jesse has become a core part of the server’s identity. He rarely wins, but that’s never stopped him from trying again. His most iconic line — "Dumb, bitch" — is delivered with a mix of frustration and deadpan flair, usually after losing a game or a bet he never should’ve taken.

Background

Jesse joined the Discord on May 20, 2021. From the beginning, he positioned himself as the gambler of the group — taking risks in games, in arguments, and in VC antics. His luck, however, remains legendarily bad. Over time, his losses have become a running joke, yet somehow also a badge of honor.

Personality

Jesse thrives in chaos. He’s unpredictable, loud when he chooses to be, and often the source of both the funniest and most frustrating moments in a single night. As a Lurker, he often appears without warning — typing something inflammatory, joining a call, then vanishing back into the void.

Online Presence

Jesse doesn’t stream often, but when he does, it’s typically filled with reckless decisions and hilarious self-destruction. Whether he's betting channel points or instigating arguments, his Twitch behavior mirrors his Discord persona: chaotic neutral with a splash of cursed energy.
